Learn About Common Crops

Rice

Rice is a staple food for more than half of the world's population. It thrives in warm climates with plenty of water.

  • Ideal Temperature: 20-30°C
  • Soil Type: Clay or loam with high moisture retention
  • Growing Season: Kharif season (June to November)

Corn

Corn, also known as maize, is a versatile crop grown for food, fodder, and industrial uses.

  • Ideal Temperature: 21-27°C
  • Soil Type: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Growing Season: Summer or early spring

Wheat

Wheat is one of the world's most important cereal crops, used primarily for bread-making and other food products.

  • Ideal Temperature: 10-24°C
  • Soil Type: Well-drained clay loam
  • Growing Season: Rabi season (November to April)
